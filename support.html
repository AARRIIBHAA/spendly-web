Frequently asked questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open the Settings app on your device, tap your name, then tap Subscriptions, select Spendly, and choose Cancel Subscription. You'll keep Premium access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I delete my account?

Open Spendly, go to Settings, and select Delete Account. This permanently removes your account and all associated data, including your expenses and receipt images.

I forgot my password, what do I do?

On the sign-in screen, tap "Forgot password?" and follow the instructions sent to your email to reset it.

The app is not recognising my receipt, what should I do?

Make sure the receipt is well-lit, flat, and fully in frame when you take the photo. If it still isn't recognised, you can always log the expense manually, and feel free to let us know so we can improve receipt scanning.

How do I contact support?

Email us at [support@spendly.app] and we'll get back to you.

We aim to respond to all support queries within 2 business days.
