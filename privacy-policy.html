Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 July 2026

Spendly ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have over your data.

What data we collect

  • Your name and email address
  • Expense data you log or import (amounts, categories, dates, merchants)
  • Categories and budgets you set up
  • Receipt images you scan for processing

How we store it

Your data is stored using Supabase, a cloud infrastructure provider, in data centres located in the EU (Frankfurt, Germany). Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Receipt images are processed to extract expense details but are not stored on our servers — they remain on your device.

Third parties we share data with

Google Gemini API — receipt images you scan are sent to Google's Gemini API to extract expense details automatically, and summaries of your spending data are sent to generate AI insights. Spendly currently uses the free tier of the Gemini API, which means that, under Google's standard terms for unpaid API services, Google may use data submitted to the API to improve its products and services.

RevenueCat — we use RevenueCat to manage Spendly Premium subscriptions. RevenueCat receives purchase and subscription status information. Your payment card details are handled entirely by Apple and are never shared with RevenueCat or with us.

Subscription data

Payment processing for Spendly Premium is handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive, process, or store your payment card details or other payment information.

Your rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time directly from within the app (Profile → Delete Account). You may also contact us to exercise any other rights you have over your personal information, including access, correction, or objection to processing.

Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your personal information, expense data, and receipt images are deleted immediately.

POPIA compliance

Spendly is committed to complying with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa. For the purposes of POPIA, Spendly is the "responsible party" for your personal information. We process personal information lawfully, only for the purposes described in this policy, and take reasonable technical and organisational measures to keep it secure. You have the right to access, correct, and delete your personal information. If you believe your rights under POPIA have not been respected, you may lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, contact us at hello@getspendly.co.za.
