Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 July 2026

Spendly ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have over your data.

What data we collect

  • Your name and email address
  • Expense data you log or import (amounts, categories, dates, merchants)
  • Receipt images you upload for processing

How we store it

Your data is stored using Supabase, a cloud infrastructure provider with data centres located in the EU/US region. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Third parties

We use the Gemini API by Google to process receipt images via optical character recognition (OCR), so that expense details can be extracted automatically. Receipt images are shared with this service solely for this purpose.

Subscription data

Payment processing for Spendly Premium is handled entirely by Apple. We do not receive, process, or store your payment card details or other payment information.

Your rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time directly from within the app. You may also contact us to exercise any other rights you have over your personal information, including access, correction, or objection to processing.

Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your personal information, expense data, and receipt images are deleted immediately.

POPIA compliance

Spendly is committed to complying with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa. We process personal information lawfully, only for the purposes described in this policy, and take reasonable technical and organisational measures to keep it secure. If you believe your rights under POPIA have not been respected, you may lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, contact us at [privacy@spendly.app].
